FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with INNERLUXES and our software development process.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with startups, SMEs, and large enterprises across a wide range of industries including fintech, healthtech, edtech, e-commerce, and more. Our clients span 25+ countries, and we adapt our process to fit organizations of any size and stage.

How is INNERLUXES different from other agencies?

Three things set us apart: we use only senior talent, we eliminate all middlemen so you talk directly to your engineers, and we operate with total transparency — sharing progress, challenges, and decisions openly throughout your project.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes. We offer dedicated maintenance and support packages to ensure your software stays up-to-date, secure, and performant after launch. We see ourselves as a long-term partner, not a one-time vendor.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. A typical MVP can range from 6–12 weeks, while larger enterprise projects may span several months. During our free consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline estimate tailored to your specific requirements.

Can you work with our existing development team?

Absolutely. We are experienced in staff augmentation and collaborative development models. Our engineers can integrate seamlessly with your existing team, adopt your workflows, and contribute meaningfully from day one.
